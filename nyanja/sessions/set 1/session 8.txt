## Munthu wavala malaya amizere ndipo wagwira nthambo pa phiri.	
## Mzibambo wacicepele ndi malaya oyera ndi jizi la buluu akwera mwala.	
## Okwera myala wabvala citsote colimba ndi nyale yamutu ndipo ndiwogeidwa kuthambo pa mwala.	
## Ka mtsikana kasewera ndi kadoli pa kasupe wagulu.	
## Gulu wakuda atawa kumbali kwa gombe ndipo ajumpa pa thanks locinjilizila.	
## Anthu akudya m'malo odyeramo ali ndi thanks ndi denga lagalasi.	
## Mzimai waimilira nyumba yosamanga bwino ili lozeguka pamwamba ndipo mutu wake wapitilila posegukapo pamwamba.	
## Mbalame lakunda ndi kuyera laimilira  pa zanja la munthu kukamwa kwake kuli mbeu la mpenyadzuwa.	
## Anyamata ampira adikila kumbali kwa mzere mocaila tenesi pomwe apenyerera mtsikana wacaya mpira.	
## Galu ofiira atawa mchanga.	
## Mzibambo adutsa mseu ndipo patali muona nyumba ilimagidwa.	
## Wa zaukatswili umodzi wabvala ma gilavisi a buluu ndi malaya a vesiti ofiira, ndi cojambulira cayanganitsidwa mogojetsa kalavani ali ndi dzuwa laselo ku mwendo kwake.	
## Mwana ayenda m'mazi pomwe akutsogoleredwa ndi munthu wina.	
## Anthu ambiri ayenda pa kanjira kumbali kwa mpanda m'madziko onse ali pansi pa makumbi.	
## Ka galu kamtundu ndi matu akulu katawa m' mauzu.	
## Mzimai atamanga munjira yamyendo kumalo okwezeka amauzu.	
## Mzibambo makabundula ofiira akhala mosamala  pa cinthu ca buluu ndi manja ake.	
## Galu ofiira, kunda ndi yera atamanga m'munda waukulu ndi kamwa kake kotsegula.	
## Ka mtsikana kavala malaya osambila apinki kasamba pa dziwe.	
## Mzibambo wavala zoyera, thambo lachikausu pansi lagwira mahando ya njinga.	
## Mwana ndi wacikulile, wacikulile mwina akuyeserera mocitila zinthu kuphunzitsa mwana.	
## Mtsikana acita masewera ojikilila pa zanja pa befi ndi mashitibedi a buluu.	
## Azibambo amajakete yong'azimila acitetedzo akusewenze pa mseu ndi makoni alalanje.	
## Mzibambo mu jizi nd jakete la citetezo aseweza pansi pajerwa.	
## Azibambo awiri mu malaya omangira asewenza kumbali kwa mseu ndi cisulo.	
## Mzibambo waimilira pa mchanga woyera ndipo wangwira coikapo myendo mu chisanu.	
## Kaswili wazomanga waimilira pa mtenje wa nyumba wavala malaya avesiti  lalanje ndi citsote colimba  ca cikhausu.	
## Mzibambo wamalaya acikhausu ndi kabudula wamutundu acaya gofu pabwalo pomwe alupenyereredwa ndi ena.	
## Mtsikana waciyera ndi bande was cikhausu mu yunifomo yakalati aimilira kutsogolo kwa chitseko ndipo wanyamula chiko cacikulu.	
## Mnyamata wagwera pa dziwe ndipo mzibambo ndi mtsikana apenyerera.	
## Anthu pa galimoto ya ATV ndi mitututu yadothi ayenda mkanjira munda ozegulukidwa ndi mitengo.	
## Mzimai wavala zofiira ajuwa cakudya cocedwa hambaga pakati ndi mpeni opatalala pomwe atsegula kati nutseka dilowa ndi zanja lina.	
## Mzimai wavala malaya oyera atali manja ayenda pa Nyanja yaikulu ndi nkongono zake zili madzi.	
## Mzibambo wavala suti awerenga pepala pomwe waimilira patsogolo pa cosema co onetsa malisece.	
## Kamwana kavala zovala zoyera kasekerera pomwe mzimai akatandiza ugwendeza ndembela.	
## Mzibambo waimilira pa kayangalala kang'ono ndi ndembela 	
## Mzibambo wacicepele wavala jakete kumbuyo kwa kauutala mu sitolo la zamyala la mitengo wapatali kenaka akuyanga patali.	
## Mzibambo wacikulile wakhala ndipo apumula pa konde pa nyumba adobe pomwe zinjinga zambiri patsidwa. 	
## Okwatilana sopanopa akujambuliwa pabwalo pa masitepisi a nyumba pomwe anthu akuyenda-yenda pa masitepisi.	
## Gulu la ana licaya mpira m'munda momwe mili madzi m'madziko	
## Mzimai wamalaya alalanje ndi zskunda ajumpa ndi manja ake kutsogolo Nyanja yaikulu pa gombe.	
## Azibambo awiri asewenza pamodzi kulumikiza ma thabwa awiri muciphinda copalila mathabwa.	
## Gulu la anthu aimilira pabwalo pa zosangalatsa, pafupi ndi galimoto yocedwa Green Jungle.	
## Atsikana awiri atsitsi lofiirira ndi mzimai was tsitsi lalifupi asekerera.	
## Mzibambo wavala suit yamalonda ali pa foni ndipo wanyamula chikwama ayenda kumbali kwa nyumba yagalasi.	
## Mzimai ndi malaya apinki  ndi jizi labuluu ayenda mseu wina wotanganidwa pomwe aligwirilile kapu was Kofi.	
## Mnyamata ometa tsitsi ndi malaya akola adothi wapakatila nkuku mkwapata mwake.
